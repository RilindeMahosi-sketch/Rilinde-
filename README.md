### Sitemap Explanation

The Mahosi Mobile Accessories website consists of 5 pages:

- **Home (index.html):** Landing page with hero banner, featured product categories, and WhatsApp button.
- **Products (products.html):** Complete product catalog with categories and pricing.
- **Specials (promotions.html):** Weekly deals and limited-time promotions.
- **About (about.html):** Business story, core values, and operating hours.
- **Contact (contact.html):** Two locations (Soweto and Braamfontein), contact form, and WhatsApp link.

All pages are linked through a navigation menu in the header.
